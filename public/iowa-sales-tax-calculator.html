What is the sales tax in Iowa?

The statewide rate is 6%. With local taxes included, the average combined rate is 6.94% as of July 1, 2026, though your exact rate depends on your city and county.

How much is sales tax on a $100 purchase in Iowa?

At the average combined rate of 6.94%, sales tax on a $100 purchase in Iowa is $6.94, for a total of $106.94.

Does Iowa have local sales taxes?

Yes. Local sales taxes in Iowa average 0.94% on top of the state rate, and the exact rate varies by city and county.
